DEXENDALIA'S POKEDEX — SINGLE-FILE WEBSITE

Upload only index.html to the top level of your GitHub repository.

Then:
1. Open repository Settings.
2. Open Pages.
3. Under Source choose Deploy from a branch.
4. Choose main and /(root).
5. Tap Save.

Your address will be:
https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/Dexendalia-s-Pokedex/

Open that address in Chrome and choose Add to Home screen.

Your trainer and Pokémon records are stored locally on your phone, so use the app's Export Backup button periodically.
